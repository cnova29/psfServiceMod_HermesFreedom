Follow the instructions on http://wiki.psfreedom.com/wiki/N900

Type 'make n900' to build the target for the N900

Copy the files 'psfreedom.ko', 'n900-n8x0/psfreedom-enable-maemo.sh' and 'n900-n8x0/psfreedom-disable-maemo.sh' to /root on your phone.
SSH to your phone as root (or rootsh through the terminal), and type ./psfreedom-enable-maemo.sh to enable PSFreedom.
Once you are done, run ./psfreedom-disable-maemo.sh to reset your device to its original behavior.
